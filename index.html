Private Pontoon and Captain Rentals

At Explore Flathead we love the beautiful lakes of The Flathead Valley and want to share the joy they have brought us for so many years. Book with us for a beautiful lake adventure and fun for the whole family!


Many Lakes to Explore

Flathead Lake, Whitefish Lake, Hungry Horse, Tally Lake or Still-water Lake. We can meet you at any of these lakes.

Your Own Crew

Take advatage of a crew that will prepare the boat for you as well as guide it.

Fun for All

Swimming, tubing, island exploring or just ancored in the middle of the lake. There is fun for the whole family.
